April 2015 - July 2015

OnMyWay Mobile App Idea

Berlin - Easter

I was at my friend Pia’s place talking about the semester’s topic: making an app to improve the experience of Berlin. Unfortunately, we had a more imminent problem — at one o’clock in the morning we realised we didn’t have any avocado for our elaborate breakfast plans. Yikes! In the entire city there were only two supermarkets open, both an hour away. So I thought…

Wouldn’t it be awesome if there was an app that could connect me with someone going that way anyway? Needless to say, there wasn’t. And we never got our avocado. But the idea for OnMyWay was born…

Self-Driving Services

What do you do when your company is facing an environmental scandal that’s all over the media? For VW it meant hiring the Macromedia team of eight to provide them with crazy ideas that connect two seemingly unrelated topics: cars and health. Their primary goal was to set a new direction for the future.

Self-driving cars were an exciting new breakthrough at the time. It seemed like every major company was working on them. That is where our design research started from.

Food Traceability System

Do you know where the fresh foods you buy are coming from? As it turns out, not that many people do. Coming from a family that produces fruits and vegetables, it was only natural to decide upon a topic that would try to solve this problem.

This project proved to be more complex than any other I had done. I worked with two different mentors that pushed me to develop both the scientific research and the practical design project. A particular challenge was balancing the needs and wants of four different target users.
